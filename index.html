Introduction

Being an avid Supreme collector/reseller myself, I always found myself browsing forums to see which items still haven't released yet and which items were popular. I made this app because I didn't see anything similar on the App Store and I felt that it would be a great app to start, considering that there are already several websites that do this.

Screenshots

Key Features

  • Authentication for users to manage their personal accounts from varying devices.
  • Browse through items sorted by release season and category.
  • Upvote/downvote items and see overall vote results in realtime.
  • Item favoriting so users won't have to look for their favorite items each time.

Future Features

  • Push notifications so users can see when their favorite items are expected to drop.
  • An in-app marketplace for users to post listings for items they have for sale or items they are looking for.
